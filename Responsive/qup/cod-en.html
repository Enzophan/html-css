What's Cash on Delivery?

Cash on Delivery allows your recipient to pay for their items upon delivery.

How does it work?

Our drivers will pay you in cash when they pick up your items. Driver will then collect the same amount from your recipients once they've received the items.

How do I use it?

In Package information page, turn on the "Cash on Delivery" toggle and then type the amount you'd like your driver to collect from your recipient.

What's Cash on Delivery?

Cash on Delivery allows your customer to pay for their items upon delivery.

How does it work?

First, you go to the merchant's place and pay for the order on behalf of your customer.

Then, deliver the order to your customer and collect the payment from them.
